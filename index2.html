About Us

MALANG is an Indian Sufi & Ethnic rock band with a complete ensemble of a Vocalist, 2 guitarists, 1 bassist and drummer.

Our music embodies “Music, Expression, Freedom” -a fusion of classic rock with Urdu/Hindi lyrics.

The music resounds with contemporary references and an informed understanding of the two-faced times we live in
